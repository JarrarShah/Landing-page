The DiscMaster™series of Spraying Machines has been designed as the versatile system of choice for small to medium-scale businesses. PortaDisc™

The Portadisc™ System offers a 'Top Only' spraying capability in situations where space or an existing conveyor line limits the use of the mainstream DiscMaster™ or DiscMatic™ Spraying Systems. By incorporating key design components from the DiscMatic™ System library, a highly flexible range of 'Top Only' spray systems, collectively known as "PORTADISC," can be configured.
