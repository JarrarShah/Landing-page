Our mission

Our mission is to free manufacturing and engineering companies of the headache of having to market and sell their products and services. By providing an outsourced sales solution we give companies back their time to focus on ‘real work’.

Every company wants to ensure their valuable products and services are sold. But having run a manufacturing and engineering business ourselves we know how difficult that can be. The tremendous time and effort that you need to put in just to make a single sale can be overwhelming. We decided to do something about it.

Just like any manufacturing or engineering process we looked at how our sales pipeline could be optimised and automated. Through multiple iterations, we found the best approach to get maximum sales results for the least amount of resources spent.

We now deliver the same optimised sales process to your business. Partner with us and we will put your sales on autopilot!

We have grown the sales of other manufacturing and engineering companies including our own.

We know how difficult it is to grow sales when you have very little time. With Outsourced Sales, your sales growth becomes independent of the time you have. Now you can spend time doing whatever you want.

  • Sleep soundly knowing that without lifting a finger your products and services will be sold.

  • Remove the hassle of training sales staff or managing extra people.

  • Get paid what you deserve without having to haggle on price or discount to compete.

  • Grow your business without having to know about the latest marketing and sales tactics.

  • Focus on the work that you enjoy the most so that you can deliver the greatest amount of value.
