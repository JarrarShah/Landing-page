Supporting our Spinning Disc
Installations around the World

Saturn Spraying has been manufacturing and supplying Spinning Disc Spraying Machines around the world since the late 1980s. Unique in our ability to offer a range of services, including bespoke design, we have installations in the main food manufacturing countries within Asia, South America, and Australia.

Our strategy when dealing with long distances, language, and culture differences has always been to provide support through the very best training at the time of installation. This, in turn, instills an advanced level of confidence in future installations. We deliver a personal service that enables us to build and establish valuable working relationships with the individuals who provide the day-to-day maintenance on-site.

Through taking this customer-focused approach, we can respond quicker to the problems being experienced, diagnosing faults and operating errors, as well as recommending the procedures and spares necessary to restore full operation.

The Spinning Disc Spraying machine is not a complex machine as it uses industry standard engineering "building blocks" that an experienced engineer will be comfortable with.

Systems such as:

  • Inverter/Motor combination sets
  • Conveyors
  • Motors
  • Pumps
  • Gearboxes


Additionally, there is specialist technology within our systems that would be explained in detail, ensuring transparency through inclusion and offering insight and support every step of the way.

During the initial phase of the build program, we provide support with locally held spares from our recommended spares list, which details the recommended spares holding items and quantities.
